StockSmith

Inventory, production, and order management for a small maker business.

  • Materials & purchasing — raw material stock, purchase orders, weighted-average costing, low-stock alerts
  • Products & builds — bills of materials, variants, bundles, and tracking finished-goods stock as it's built
  • Orders & allocation — reserving on-hand or soon-to-be-built stock against incoming orders through to shipment
  • Etsy sync — pulling shop orders and keeping listing quantities in sync with real inventory
This is a private, single-store tool — not a public product or service. It's used exclusively by its owner to run their own Etsy shop.
